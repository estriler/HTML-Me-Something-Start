Super Market Simulator


This has been my latest obsession over the last few weeks and overall the biggest inpiration for my Candy Store Simulator idea. I don't know the psychology behind it, all I know is that it feeds the dopamine machine something fearce.

Stardew Valley


I'm more inspired by the art style and asthetics of Stardew Valley that the actual game mechanics of it. The pixel graphics and pleasing sound effects are going to be a crutial part of my Candy Story Simulator game.
